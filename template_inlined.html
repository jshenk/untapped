Busted brackets or not, Spring is (finally) coming in hot and this is how fantastic we're feeling about what we have lined up for our April event: We will be joining the Healthy Harbor Initiative and Baltimore Community ToolBank (1224 Wicomico St.) for a "Professor Trash Wheel Dumpster Dive" (don't worry - you won't be required to actually enter the dumpster, unless you're into that kind of thing) to analyze the contents and sort out the recyclables from Professor Trash Wheel, a unique habitat restoration project.

When: Saturday, April 14, 2018
10:00 AM to 1:00 PM (EDT) 1224

Where: Wicomico St, Baltimore, MD 21230

Professor Trash Wheel and her friend, Mr. Trash Wheel, have collected over 1.5 million pounds of trash, making Baltimore a cleaner place for all of us; and in April we will have an opportunity to contribute to this important project. If you have not seen them, they are floating trash interceptors that use solar and water energy to collect litter and debris in Baltimore's Inner Harbor. Findings from the event are used to help pass legislation aimed at reducing litter such as the foam ban recently passed by City Council. Check out this video posted by CNN. Kind of a big deal. So by association, we will all be kind of a big deal too.
